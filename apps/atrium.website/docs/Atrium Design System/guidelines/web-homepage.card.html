Good questions

Frequently asked questions

What does Atrium actually do?+
We're a full creative & marketing studio for hospitality — brand, design, photography, web, email, SEO, and social. One human team, one cohesive brand, everywhere your guests find you.
How is this different from a traditional agency?+
No bloated retainers or slow rounds. We embed with your team, work from a living design system, and turn brand guidelines into real assets fast — menus, ads, films, and full campaigns.
Do you only work with restaurants?+
Restaurants are our home, but we work across hospitality — hotels, cafés, bars, and food brands. If guests experience it, we can brand and grow it.
How do we get started?+
Book a call. We'll run a short brand teardown, align on goals, and propose a scope — whether that's a single project or an embedded creative partnership.
