Wide letter-spaced caps kicker — sits above headings as a section eyebrow ("THE EXPERIENCE ERA").

```jsx
<Eyebrow tone="amber">Media and Advertising</Eyebrow>
<Eyebrow tone="onDark" spread="widest">The Experience Era</Eyebrow>
```

`tone`: `muted | teal | mint | amber | onDark`. `spread`: `wide` (0.18em) or `widest` (0.32em).
